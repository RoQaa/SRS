      About Us

      Gazala Steel Fabrication, GSF, is a SESCOTRANS subsidiary that introduced its services to the steel fabrication market in 2007. We are striving to lead the steel fabrication market. We are determined to succeed and to maximize the value provided to our partners both in Egypt and internationally. Since steel is majorly a support component, we raise your trust and support your investments which allows us together to aim for a better future, better environment and to achieve the great success we all look for. We are addicted to perfection, as the only requirement and the major standard we target. That’s why we have carefully designed a whole work culture and attitude centered around agility, precision, attention to details, reliability and environmentally friendly manufacturing.

      A Wide Range Of Diversified Product

      GSF offers a wide range of diversified products that meet clients’ requirements, products include but are not limited to, Structural Steel, Semi-Trailers, Conveyor Belts, Packing Machines, Vibrating Screens, etc., serving several industries e.g., Construction, Power, Oil & Gas, Cement and aggregate, Chemical, Petro Chemical and Transportation” with an annual production capacity that exceeds 35,000 Tonnes.

      A Value-driven Organization

      GSF is a value-driven organization, starting from our recruitment philosophy to our customer service; we aim to demonstrate our values in every interaction. We are constantly moving forward and evolving. It is crucial we continue to live, learn and embrace our values into the future.

      Vision

      Striving For Excellence Since 2007

      At Gazala Steel Fabrication, we strive to stand as a beacon of excellence and innovation in the global steel fabrication market. We are committed to sustainable growth and unwavering reliability, serving as trusted partners to our clients globally.

      Mission

      Providing Innovative Fabrication Solutions

      At Gazala Steel Fabrication, we engineer and deliver superior steel solutions that empower our clients to build with confidence and efficiency. Guided by a relentless pursuit of excellence, we leverage our expertise and innovation to craft bespoke products and services that exceed industry standards and client expectations.
